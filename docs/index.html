Guarantees

Four things that cannot be broken.

The bank cannot trace a payment

The bank signs coins without seeing what it signs. At deposit, it has no way to connect the payment to the withdrawal. Not a setting. Enforced by the mathematics.

Coins cannot be forged

Every coin carries a signature from the issuing bank. No valid signature means no value. Creating a fake coin is not possible without the bank's private key.

Spending a coin twice is caught

The bank records every coin deposited. A second deposit of the same coin is rejected immediately, at any branch, at any participating bank.

Regulators still have what they need

digicash runs inside a licensed bank. The bank stays regulated and auditable. Privacy for customers does not mean invisibility for regulators.
